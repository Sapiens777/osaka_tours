Frequently Asked Questions

Are food and drinks included in the tour price?

To keep the experience 100% flexible to your tastes and dietary requirements, food and drinks are not included. We will stop at local spots, and you only pay for what you choose to consume.

I don't speak any Japanese. Is that a problem?

Not at all! I am fluent in English and currently studying 6 other languages. I will act as your cultural bridge and translator, and I can be your teacher!

Can we customize the shopping route?

Absolutely. Once you book, we will have a quick chat about your style (e.g., 90s vintage, luxury brands, or streetwear), and I will tailor the exact shops we visit in Nakazakicho or Amerikamura just for you.
